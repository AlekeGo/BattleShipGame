THE DIFFERENTIATOR

A coach that cites
your actual moves.

Most game AIs give you generic tips. Ocean Strike's Coach reads every shot you made — sequence, timing, density — and writes you a one-page report after each match.

It tracks shot entropy, parity discipline, post-hit follow-through, and placement tendencies. Then it tells you, in plain language, the one habit costing you the most games.

WHAT'S DIFFERENT

01

You came to play Battleship. So play Battleship. The core game, the Coach report, all 10×10 grids — free, forever, no banner blocking row B.
